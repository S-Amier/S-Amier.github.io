    • Characters

    In short, Final Fantasy X follows the epic story of a summoner named Yuna and her quest to destroy Sin. You must guide the main character Tidus, through the many events of the game. We introduce here the characters that accompany Yuna along her journey. Note that each character has a unique area of expertise. Review their strengths so you can use them to your adventage on the battlefield.

    Tidus

    Tidus is a cheerful, rising blitzball star playing for the Zanarkand Abes. He has long resented his father, who was a renowned player himself before his untimely death. Tidus's quick moves allow him to attack even the swiftest foes with ease.

    Wiki for Tidus.

    Yuna

    Daughter of High Summoner Braska. Honest and determined. Yuna embarks on a pilgramige to obtain the Final Aeon and defeat Sin. Yuna is learning the mystical art of summoning aeons - powerful spirits of yore.

    Wiki for Yuna.

    Wakka

    Coach and captain of the local blitzball team, the Besaid Aurochs. Wakka plans to retire from the sport after this year's tournament, so that the can devote himself fully to serving as Yuna's guardian. His deadly blitzball is especially useful for shooting down aerial enemies.

    Wiki for Wakka.

    Lulu

    One of Yuna's guardians. She and Wakka think of Yuna as a younger sister. Lulu's stoic and self-possessed nature makes her appear insensitive at times. She specialises in the art of black magic.

    Wiki for Lulu.

    Kimahri

    A young warrior of the Ronso tribe, Kimahri has watched over Yuna from her youngest days. He speaks little, but is deeply devoted to Yuna and serves her loyally as a guardian. Kimahri can learn enemy skills with his Lancet ability.

    Wiki for Kimahri.

    Auron

    The legendary guardian who, together with High Summoner Braska, defeated Sin ten years before. A man of few words, he guides Yuna and Tidus on their mission to vanquish Sin once more. He swings his gigantic sword with such power that even the toughest fiends are cut asunder.

    Wiki for Auron.

    Rikku

    A young Al Bhed girl. Her personality is upbeat and positive, and she is not afraid to speak her mind. She works hard to restore her outcast people to their former glory. Rikku handles mechanical devices with ease, and can steal items from enemies too.

    Wiki for Rikku.
